NASA Data Sources

OPTIMAL: Landsat + Sentinel-2 Combined

Effective Revisit: ~3 days
Resolution: 10-30m multi-scale
Spectral Bands: 11-13 bands including red-edge
Archive: 1972-present (Landsat program)

Advantages:

  • Maximum temporal coverage
  • Multi-scale spatial analysis
  • Species discrimination (red-edge)
  • Long-term trend analysis
  • 97% wildflower detection accuracy
Sentinel-2A/2B

Resolution: 10m (visible/NIR), 20m (red-edge)
Revisit: 5 days
Bands: 13 (includes red-edge)
Accuracy: 97% wildflower detection
Best for: High-res detail, species ID

Landsat 8/9

Resolution: 30m
Revisit: 8 days (combined)
Bands: 11 (includes coastal aerosol)
Archive: 1972-present
Best for: Trends, regional coverage

GLOBE Observer + Ground Truth

Type: Citizen science ground observations
Update: Real-time
Use: Validation and training data
Integration: Complements satellite data
